New York Yankees vs New York Mets

By Hanny Wolkoff

Below, you will find three charts depicting the differences between the New York Yankees and the New York Mets from March to October in the years 2017-2022 in the categories of Attendane at each ballpark, Earned Run Average (ERA), and home-runs hit per year. The Yankees and the Mets are two of the most dominant teams in baseball currently, and both had one of the most competitive seasons this past year. Since they are both located in New York, the teams are constantly being compared to one another and a historic rivalry has emerged.

Please note, the year 2020 has been left off of these graphs as the season was cut short due to COVID-19.
